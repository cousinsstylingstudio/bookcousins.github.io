Two Ways to Host Us

We work around your facility. Choose whichever setup fits your space.

Option 1: Mobile Unit
Our mobile unit arrives at your facility at the scheduled time.
Employees access services during breaks or shift transitions without leaving the property.
◆16 feet of accessible parking space
◆Standard vehicle access to your lot
◆A designated point of contact to manage participant scheduling
◆Pre-deployment brief shared with your facilities coordinator in advance
Option 2: In-Facility Setup
Our team establishes a portable service station at your location.
A break room, open floor, or designated wellness space is sufficient.
◆Space for one to three stations
◆Standard electrical outlets
