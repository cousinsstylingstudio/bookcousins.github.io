What You Will Find Here

Cousins is not for everyone. It is for professionals who want to do good work, be treated with respect, and not spend their energy managing every part of the business themselves. If that sounds like you, keep reading.

A professional environment with clear expectations
Flexible ways to contribute: studio, walk-in, mobile, and events

Who We Are Looking For

Professionals who care about their work.

We are looking for professionals who care about the quality of their work, how they communicate, and how clients feel in their chair. This environment is best for people who want flexibility but still respect structure, preparation, and accountability.

What Does Not Belong Here
◆Ego or behavior that disrupts the team or the client experience
◆Rudeness or hostility toward clients or colleagues
◆Resistance to feedback or clear communication
◆Treating clients as transactions rather than people in your care
Strong craft, held to a consistent standard
Technical skills matter here. So does showing up the same way every time, regardless of who the client is.
A learner's posture
You take feedback as information. You ask clarifying questions. You approach new environments with curiosity, not defensiveness.
Proactive communication
On a mobile deployment, the team depends on each person to communicate clearly and keep the service running. Going silent is not an option.
Genuine care for the person in your chair
How someone feels during the experience is part of the service. Not separate from it.

How It Works

A straightforward process.
